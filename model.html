Definition of Model

The Model is the component of MVC architecture that manages application data and business logic. It is responsible for storing, retrieving, and modifying data.

Responsibilities of the Model

  • Manage application data.
  • Store and retrieve information.
  • Update and modify data.
  • Perform business logic and calculations.
  • Communicate with databases or data storage systems.

Examples of Data Managed by the Model

  1. User account information
  2. Student records
  3. Product details
  4. Order information
  5. Payment records
  6. Employee details
